AbdelHalim Hafez

Abdel Halim is considered to be one of the greatest Egyptian musicians of his era, along with Umm Kulthum, Mohamed Abdel Wahab, Farid Al Atrach, Mohamed Fawzi, and Shadia.[2][6] As his popularity grew, he was given the nickname 'el-Andaleeb el-Asmar (Arabic: العندليب الأسمر),[7][8][9] meaning The Dark-Skinned Nightingale.[10] He achieved extraordinary success as a popular musician throughout the Arab world, with estimated sales of over 80 million records.
